Fried Rice

This recipe is one made from love and great ingredients.

I hope you enjoy this dish as much as my stomach did.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Chicken breast
  • Chicken Broth
  • Frozen stir-fry veges
  1. Cut all vegetables and set side
  2. Fry ginger and garlic 1-2 mins
  3. Add vegetables to fry-pan with oil, high heat
  4. Add meat and egg
  5. Serve when hot
