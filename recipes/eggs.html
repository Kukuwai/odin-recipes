Exciting Eggs

Fried eggs

This recipe is one made from love and great ingredients.

I hope you enjoy this dish as much as my stomach did.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Water
  1. Crack eggs into an oiled pan
  2. Wait till eggs are partially cooked through then add a splash of water, put lid on
  3. When egg tops are cooked, remove eggs
  4. Serve on your favourite substrate, such as toast
