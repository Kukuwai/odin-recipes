Spicy Tomatoe Soup

Tomatoe Soup

This recipe is one made from love and great ingredients.

I hope you enjoy this dish as much as my stomach did.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Paprika
  • Vegetable stock
  • Cream
  1. Cook all vegetables together
  2. Add stock, cook till veges tender
  3. Blend all vegetables with stock
  4. Bring blended soup back onto low heat
  5. Add cream and serve
